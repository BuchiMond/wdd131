Site Plan — BigBrandCity

Site Name

BigBrandCity represents creativity, innovation, and excellence in the world of design. The name reflects a digital hub for individuals and businesses looking to enhance their brand identity through graphics, web design, and corporate branding services. I chose this name because it captures the vision of creating “big brands” that stand out in a competitive city-like marketplace.

Site Purpose

The purpose of BigBrandCity is to showcase creative design solutions for businesses and entrepreneurs. The website will offer information about our design services, including graphic design, website creation, and full corporate branding. It will also serve as a source of inspiration for clients looking to refresh or launch a brand identity that stands out in today’s digital world.

Scenarios

  • Q: How can BigBrandCity help my business create a unique brand identity?
    A: We offer complete branding packages that include logo design, colour palette creation, brand strategy, and marketing materials tailored to your business goals.
  • Q: Do you also design websites that are mobile friendly?
    A: Yes, all our websites are designed to be responsive, ensuring they look great on both mobile and desktop devices.
  • Q: Can I get a logo redesign for my existing brand?
    A: Absolutely. We specialise in refreshing outdated logos while maintaining your brand’s identity and core values.
  • Q: How long does it take to complete a website project?
    A: Depending on the complexity, most projects take between 2 to 4 weeks. We keep our clients updated throughout every stage of the process.

Colour Scheme

The selected colours create a bold, elegant, and professional feel suitable for a branding and design website.

  • #53381D — headings and accents.
  • #FFCC00 — highlights and buttons.
  • #E6CB96 — background and section contrast.

Typography

The site uses Roboto from Google Fonts for all text. This modern, clean font enhances readability and professionalism.
