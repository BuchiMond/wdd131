BigBrandCity started as a one-person studio and now supports local businesses with identity, web and marketing design that works.

Our story

Founded by Buchi Vitalis, BigBrandCity began with a strong desire to help small and growing businesses look professional without spending huge budgets. Over the years, we’ve built a reputation for blending creativity with strategy, focusing on measurable results, consistent branding, and clear communication that strengthens every client’s identity and online presence.
